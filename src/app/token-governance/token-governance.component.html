Antelope Governance

Decentralizing Governance. Resisting Censorship.

Devoted to the principles of decentralization, the Antelope ecosystem adopts a hierarchical, two-tier, and skill-based governance infrastructure comprising a Technical Council and a Business Council

The mechanism explores Conviction or Quadratic Voting systems to effectively mitigate sybil attacks, collusion, and other risk vectors that are typical of time-boxed governance procedures.

Technical Council

The TC votes on matters concerning protocol upgrades, inflation adjustments, and commission parameters. Initially appointed by the Antelope DAO, subsequent applications for council membership will be subject to a two-third consensus of the ANTLP community
