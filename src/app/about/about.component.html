3 trillion dollars supply gap

SMEs account for more than 40% of trade finance applications denied by banks. They use "informal" funding in roughly 20% of cases and "formal" alternative financing in just 15% of cases.

The global trade finance deficit is set to widen upto $ 3 trillion by the end of the decade with supply chains reallocating more and more to emerging countries with less developed financial structures.

A funding deficit in the commodity sector could only have a knock-on impact on overall trade finance markets.
